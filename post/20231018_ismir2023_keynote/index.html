Keynote at ISMIR 2023

18 October 2023 1 min read news

I feel very honored to give a keynote address at ISMIR 2023. I will address the multifaceted needs of stakeholders who rely on the technologies revolving around music information retrieval (MIR). In particular, I will zoom in examples from music recommender systems and demonstrate the potential risks of neglecting artists’ needs—and provide strategies for mitigation.
