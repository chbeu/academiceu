I'll be guiding a virtual session of WiMIR workshop 2020

10 August 2020 1 min read news

I am happy to announce that I will be guiding one of the nine virtual sessions of the WiMIR workshop 2020 at vISMIR2020. The title of my session is: The best ever recommendation & For who? And how do you know that?

I am very much looking forward to it.

Registration information will be announced soon. Follow @Women_MIR to not miss any news.

The WiMIR workshop is going virtual with nine sessions with MIR all-stars! These sessions will be spread across four weekends (and timezones) leading up to #vISMIR2020. Registration information coming soon.#WiMIR2020 #WomenInMIR #WiMIRworkshop @ismir2020

— Women in MIR (@Women_MIR) August 7, 2020
