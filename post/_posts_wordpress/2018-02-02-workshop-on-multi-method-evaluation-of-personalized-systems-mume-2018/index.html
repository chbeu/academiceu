Workshop on Multi-Method Evaluation of Personalized Systems (MuMe 2018)

2 February 2018 1 min read news

Together with Eva Zangerle and Bart Knijnenburg, I will organize a workshop on Multi-Method Evaluation of Personalized Systems (MuMe 2018) in scope of the 26th Conference on User Modeling, Adaptation and Personalization (UMAP 2018) in Singapore!
More information can soon be found on our workshop website at http://multimethods.info.

Ilknur Celik, Ilaria Torre, Frosina Koceva, Christine Bauer, Eva Zangerle, Bart Knijnenburg (2018). UMAP 2018 Intelligent User-Adapted Interfaces: Design and Multi-Modal Evaluation (IUadaptMe) Workshop Chairs' Welcome & Organization. 26th Conference on User Modeling, Adaptation and Personalization.
