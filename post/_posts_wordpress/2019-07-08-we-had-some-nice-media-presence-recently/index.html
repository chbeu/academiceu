We had some nice media presence lately

8 July 2019 1 min read news

It’s great to see our scientific results being communicated to the public. Our results were well received on the media.

And here we go, the list of articles on the media:

  1. 22 June 2019, Vorarlberger Nachrichten. Länderunterschiede im Musikgeschmack (in German).
  2. 21 June 2019, studium.at. Linzer Forscher zeigen große Länderunterschiede im Musikgeschmack auf (in German).
  3. 21 June 2019, futurezone. Linzer Forscher analysieren Musikgeschmack in verschieden Ländern (in German).
  4. 21 June 2019, APA. Linzer Forscher zeigen große Länderunterschiede im Musikgeschmack auf (in German).
  5. 21 June 2019, Wiener Zeitung. Musikgeschmäcker und Watschen (in German).
  6. 21 June 2019, DER STANDARD. Linzer Forscher zeigen Länderunterschiede im Musikgeschmack auf (in German).

And, well, also read the original PLOS ONE article (open access) on music preferences in 47 countries with respect to what is considered mainstream including recommendation approaches considering users’ mainstreaminess by Markus Schedl and me.

Christine Bauer, Markus Schedl (2019). Global and country-specific mainstreaminess measures: Definitions, analysis, and usage for improving personalized music recommendation systems. PLOS ONE.
