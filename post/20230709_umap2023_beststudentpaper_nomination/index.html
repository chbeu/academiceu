Nomination for Best Student Paper Award at UMAP 2023

9 July 2023 1 min read news



What a great acknowledgment. Our UMAP 2023 paper entitled “Amplifying artists’ voices: item provider perspectives on influence and fairness of music streaming platforms” was nominated for a Best Student Paper Award.

Congratulations to Giuseppe Spillo and team (Cataldo Musto, Marco Polignano, Pasquale Lops, Marco de Gemmis, and Giovanni Semeraro) who won the Best Student Paper Award—well deserved!



Karlijn Dinnissen, Christine Bauer (2023). Amplifying artists' voices: item provider perspectives on influence and fairness of music streaming platforms. 31st ACM Conference on User Modeling, Adaptation and Personalization (UMAP 2023).
